To Shampoo, follow these steps:

  1. Apply to wet air
  2. Massage gently into hair and scalp
  3. Rinse thoroughly

Then, to condition proceed by:

  1. Wringing excess water from air
  2. Apply conditioner and massage gently into hair from roots to ends
  3. Rinse thoroughly into warm water
